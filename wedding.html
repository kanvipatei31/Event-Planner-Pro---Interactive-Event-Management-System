Hindu Traditional Wedding

Hindu weddings are grand celebrations full of traditions, colors, music and rituals. They usually last several days and bring together large families and friends.

  • Mehendi ceremony with beautiful henna designs
  • Haldi ceremony for good luck
  • Baraat where groom arrives with music
  • Saat Phere around sacred fire
  • Grand reception celebration

Muslim Wedding (Nikah)

A Muslim wedding is known as Nikah. It is a sacred contract between bride and groom performed in the presence of family, friends and a religious leader.

  • Nikah ceremony conducted by an Imam
  • Mehr (gift) given to bride by groom
  • Beautiful traditional outfits
  • Elegant floral decorations
  • Walima reception celebration

Christian Wedding

Christian weddings usually take place in a church with beautiful white decorations, flowers and traditional music. It is a peaceful and elegant ceremony.

  • Bride walks down the aisle
  • Exchange of vows and rings
  • Blessing by priest
  • Holy communion
  • Reception with family and friends
